Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water

Recipe Instructions

  1. Get a rice cooker, you're fucked if you're trying to follow these instructions without one. Sorry!
  2. Add 1-2 cups of dry uncooked rice into the rice cooker. Be careful, your rice cooker might use a smaller-than-normal cup measurement - so you might want to use the exact cup that came with your specific rice cooker.
  3. Wash the rice
  4. Add the appropriate amount of water for the amount of rice you're cooking - yes, the amount of water fluctuates based on how much rice you're cooking
  5. Press the "cook" button or whatever the appropriate option is for your particular rice cooker and circumstance. If you have a fancy rice cooker, sorry but you'll have to figure this shit out on your own dude.
  6. Wait until your rice cooker does whatever it does to signify that it's done
